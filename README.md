# GeekTheGreyBeard

Grey beard, sharp tools, stubborn curiosity — and a long-running habit of making technology serve the people doing the real work.

I'm **Rodney Rees**, also known as **GeekTheGreyBeard**: healthcare IT director, systems and network architect, automation builder, compliance/policy wrangler, consultant, and founder. My work sits where technical depth, business judgment, and mission-critical operations meet.

For decades, I’ve helped under-resourced or fragmented environments become mature, accountable, useful technology operations — especially in rural healthcare, where the stakes are real and the margin for nonsense is small.

## What I do

- Lead and modernize healthcare IT operations for rural hospitals, clinics, and mission-driven organizations.
- Build practical automation that reduces friction, improves reliability, and gives staff time back.
- Design infrastructure across Linux, Windows, virtualization, networking, databases, dashboards, backups, and cloud/on-prem hybrids.
- Write and refine IT, security, privacy, patient-safety, downtime, compliance, and operational policies.
- Translate between executives, clinicians, vendors, technical teams, and frontline users so the work actually gets done.

## Companies and systems

### Rees Admin Services, Inc. — RASi

I founded **Rees Admin Services, Inc.** to provide compliance-focused IT leadership and directorship for rural healthcare districts and mission-driven organizations.

RASi is technically an MSP, but philosophically it is closer to an **anti-MSP**: the goal is not to keep clients dependent. The goal is to build capable local IT departments, mentor staff, modernize systems, improve compliance, optimize vendors/contracts, and make technology stop getting in the way of patient care.

### PatriciAI

**PatriciAI** is my personal automation and agentic AI system — part assistant, part lab coordinator, part memory system, part guardrail layer for the growing pile of tools, agents, services, and experiments.

It pulls together local AI, speech, retrieval, infrastructure automation, documentation, and practical agent coordination into something useful instead of just shiny.

### Splat-I

**Splat-I** is the future-facing vision: self-evolving agentic systems and adaptive intelligence services.

Brand philosophy: **Wildcard Logic. Precision Outcomes.**

Think RAG systems, fine-tuning, agents/agencies, assistant personas, wearables, workflow automation, technical automation, RPA, robot/AI integration, whole-life assistants, enterprise intelligence layers, and domain-specific custom solutions — the “Bugatti” version of adaptive intelligence for people and organizations that move differently.
