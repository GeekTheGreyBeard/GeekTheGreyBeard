**Grey beard. Sharp tools. Controlled chaos turned into useful systems.**

I'm **Rodney Rees** — healthcare IT director, systems/network architect, automation builder, compliance and policy wrangler, consultant, RASi founder, and builder of PatriciAI, Splat-I, and AdminX.

My work lives where technical depth, business judgment, regulatory pressure, and real operations collide. I help rural healthcare and other high-trust organizations turn fragmented technology, workflows, vendors, documents, and decisions into mature, accountable systems that support the people doing the real work.

## Current direction

- **RASi** — the parent authority brand: embedded leadership, control, and intelligent execution for modern operations. Healthcare is the earned proof base; the larger mission is high-accountability operations where technology, workflows, automation, governance, and decision-making have to move together.
- **Splat-I** — the operating and orchestration layer for secure automation, governed intelligence, and precision execution. **Wildcard Logic. Precision Outcomes.**
- **PatriciAI** — the public-facing interactive intelligence presence and persona framework, plus my working local-first automation, memory, voice, and agentic AI coordination system.
- **AdminX** — the automation/workflow execution layer: making recurring operational friction observable, structured, repeatable, and governable.

## Operating beliefs

- Most modernization problems are **execution problems first**.
- Automation without control is just **faster chaos**.
- Blind trust is not an AI strategy.
- Technology should never be a stumbling block between staff and the mission.
- Useful systems explain themselves before touching production.
